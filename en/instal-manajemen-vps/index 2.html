FAQ

Some frequently asked questions by clients
How many websites can I host on a VPS?

You can host as many websites as you want, the limit is the capacity of your VPS, eg storage capacity, bandwidth, RAM, and others.

If you want to subscribe to your own vps, what is a good provider?

For VPS stability, we recommend that you subscribe to major providers such as AWS Amazon, Google Cloud, Microsoft Azure, OVH Cloud, Alibaba Cloud, Linode, UpCloud, Vultr, Digital Ocean, and others.

For local providers we can recommend such as Cloud Kilat, Dewaweb, IDCloudhost, Cloudmatika, etc.

Will the VPS get a Dedicated IP?

Yes, your VPS will get a dedicated IP, either order from us, or from other providers.

What if I have a problem with a website hosted on a VPS, can I get help?

Of course, as long as your subscription is active, we will always be there to help you if there is a problem with your VPS.

Will All Websites Get SSL?

Yes, all websites on your VPS can get Let's Encrypt SSL for free, and will automatically renew if it expires.
